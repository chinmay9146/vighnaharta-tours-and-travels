Bhimashankar Wildlife Sanutary






Spread over 131 sq. kilometer Bhima Shankar wildlife sanctuary is another remarkable Maharashtra wildlife Sanctuary. Located in the Western Ghats of India, the wildlife sanctuary attracts many tourists for its biodiversity. The Sanctuary is also recognized as the biodiversity hotspots in the world. Along with mammals like barking deer, wild boar, langurs; the sanctuary also preserves many reptiles, insects, amphibians and many types of butterflies.

Best time to visit:- The best time to visit the sanctuary is between October and February.

Location:- Located at Khed Taluka of Pune District of Maharashtra.

Tour Duration:-2Nights-3days

Itinerary

Day 1

Depart from Mumbai / Pune to reach Koynanagar. On arrival, check-in to the hotel. Post Lunch, visit the Koyna Dam & Lake Tapping Project. Overnight stay at the Hotel..

Day 2

In the morning, after Breakfast, leave to visit the Ozarde waterfall and Ghat Matha Wind mill projects. Return to the hotel for lunch. In the evening, after taking rest for a while, leave for Nehru Garden and experience boating in Koyna Backwater. Overnight stay at the Hotel.

Day 3

Check-out from the Hotel after breakfast, and visit the Koyna Wildlife Sanctuary. Then depart for Mumbai / Pune. Upon reaching, get down at your place, or Airport / Bus / Railway station to carry on with your further destination. Tour Ends
