Koyna Wildlife Sanutary






        One of the must visit wildlife sanctuaries in Maharashtra is the Koyna wildlife Sanctuary. Situated in a dense forest reserve in Satara District of Maharashtra, Koyna is a beautiful sanctuary. A home to a number of mammals, such as Bengal Tigers, Indian Leopards, deer, Indian Bison, sloth bears, barking deer, and mouse deer; along with birds like Night-tailed light jar, Brown-capped pygmy woodpecker, and reptiles, Koyna is a place of sheer bliss.

Best time to visit:- The best time to visit Koyna is between June to October. October being the best month to visit.

Location:- Koyananagar, Koyna, Maharashtra 416212

Itinerary

Day 1

Depart from Mumbai / Pune to reach Koynanagar. On arrival, check-in to the hotel. Post Lunch, visit the Koyna Dam & Lake Tapping Project. Overnight stay at the Hotel..

Day 2

In the morning, after Breakfast, leave to visit the Ozarde waterfall and Ghat Matha Wind mill projects. Return to the hotel for lunch. In the evening, after taking rest for a while, leave for Nehru Garden and experience boating in Koyna Backwater. Overnight stay at the Hotel.

Day 3

Check-out from the Hotel after breakfast, and visit the Koyna Wildlife Sanctuary. Then depart for Mumbai / Pune. Upon reaching, get down at your place, or Airport / Bus / Railway station to carry on with your further destination. Tour Ends
